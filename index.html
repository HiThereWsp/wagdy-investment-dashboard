Investment Conclusion

The decline in operating profit from SAR 1,002.6M (2022) to SAR 961.0M (2023) was offset by significantly higher finance income from term deposits. While 2022 results were impacted by one-time write-offs (distribution center and investment properties), they benefited from larger impairment reversals. The 2023 profitability profile shows stable operational performance, with overall returns now supported by strategic cash management generating substantial interest income. The company maintains a healthy balance sheet with improving leverage ratios, though investors should monitor the customer concentration risk and the sustainability of finance income in varying interest rate environments.
